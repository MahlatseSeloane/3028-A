About The Website

I created Kgothatso’s Adventures for me to share my experiennces as a young South African girl living in one of the most famous hoods in the world, Soweto. These experiences are filled with feelings of joy, happiness, love as well as self doubt, confusion and hopelessness we all experience. I hope you will join me as i embark on this journey of self discovery.

About Me

Hey! My name is Kgothatso Modupe. I am 21 years old South African living in Soweto, Johannesburg. I have lived in Soweto my entire life with my Mom, Dad, and younger brother. There is a saying in my hood, and it goes, “you can take me out of the hood, but you can’t take the hood out of me”, and this personifies me to my core. No matter where I am or who I am with, I will always be the same Kgothatso that grow up in Soweto, the greatest “hood” in the world.

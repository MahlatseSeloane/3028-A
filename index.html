Hey! My name is Kgothatso Modupe.

I am a 21 years old South African living in Soweto, Johannesburg. I have lived in Soweto my entire life with my Mom, Dad, and younger brother. I created this blog for me to share my experiences as a young-adult girl coming of age. These experiences are filled with feelings of joy, happiness, love as well as self doubt, confusion and hopelessness we all experience. I hope you will join me as i embark on this journey of self discovery.
